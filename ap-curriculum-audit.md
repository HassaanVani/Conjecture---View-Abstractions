### AP Biology — 30%
9 visualizations. Covers cell division, population growth, photosynthesis, respiration, DNA, evolution, ecology. Gaps: enzymes, signal transduction, Mendelian genetics.

### AP Chemistry — 10%
10 visualizations. Covers orbitals, bonds, VSEPR, phases, stoichiometry, kinetics, enthalpy, equilibrium. Major gaps: intermolecular forces (18-22% of exam), acids/bases, entropy. Note: titration.tsx exists but route is still missing from App.tsx.
